# Canon — Mencius's Mother Moves Three Times (孟母三遷)

> Source: 中華歷史 + 民間傳說. Mencius (Mengzi 孟子 372-289 BCE) 為先秦哲學家 + 儒家思想代表. 「孟母三遷」傳說最早見於漢代《列女傳·母儀傳》(劉向 ~16 BCE), 至少 2000 年口傳 + 文獻流傳. 完全公有領域 (歷史人物 + 民俗故事, 無版權主張可能).
> **NOT used**: 任何特定譯本延伸段落 / 特定教科書 / 動畫改編 (e.g. 中華卡通總動員 / 親親文化繪本). 全 A2 自創句式.
> CEFR-A2 retelling for Pickup Ch22. 自創 plot beats only.

## 7-Beat Arc (with key vocab)

### Beat 1 · Setup — A Mother and Her Son
- Long ago in China, a mother lived with her small son.
- The boy's name was Meng. His father was gone.
- The mother wanted Meng to grow up strong and kind.
- Their first house stood near a place where people remembered family.

**Vocab**: mother / son / boy / house / first

### Beat 2 · The Burial Games
- Every day Meng watched people walk past in long white lines.
- He saw them bow. He saw them cry softly.
- Soon Meng played the same games at home with sticks.
- His mother watched. Her heart felt heavy.

**Vocab**: watch / walk / play / game / heart

### Beat 3 · Move to the Market
- "This is not the place for my son to learn," she said.
- She packed their things. They moved near a busy market.
- Meng watched people buy and sell all day long.
- Soon he played at calling out prices, just like the sellers.

**Vocab**: place / pack / move / market / sell

### Beat 4 · Calling Out Prices
- "Two for one coin! Fresh today!" Meng shouted in their yard.
- His mother heard him. She put down her cloth.
- "He learns what he sees. He sees what is near."
- She knew it was time to move again.

**Vocab**: coin / shout / yard / hear / learn

### Beat 5 · Next to the School
- The third house stood right next to a small school.
- Every morning Meng heard children reading out loud.
- He heard a teacher speak about good and kind ways.
- Meng began to copy the words. He sat with a book.

**Vocab**: third / school / morning / read / book

### Beat 6 · The Cut Cloth
- One day Meng came home early. He did not want to study.
- His mother sat at her loom. She had been weaving all day.
- Without a word, she took her knife. She cut the cloth in two.
- "Like this cloth, your study will be broken if you stop."

**Vocab**: study / loom / weave / knife / cut

### Beat 7 · The Great Thinker
- Meng did not stop again. He read every day for many years.
- He grew up and became a great thinker for all of China.
- People still tell of how his mother moved three times for him.
- A child learns most from what stands close to home.

**Vocab**: stop / grow / great / thinker / close

## Theme

- 「孩子學的是身邊的人在做什麼。」
- 媽媽不用罵, 不用打 — 她搬家, 她剪布. 用環境 + 行動教.
- 海外華人家長 #1 共鳴 (教育 / 換學區 / 為了孩子搬家) — 中華 heritage 核心故事之一.
