# Canon — The Ugly Duckling (醜小鴨)

> Hans Christian Andersen, 1843. CEFR-A2 retelling for Pickup Ch2.
> Mochi 跳上奶奶矮牆 → Hana 趴下 → 奶奶翻開「池塘那頁」說故事。

## 7-Beat Arc (with key vocab)

### Beat 1 · Setup — Mother Duck Waits
- Mother duck sits on a nest of eggs in the warm reeds.
- The other eggs all crack open — yellow ducklings tumble out.
- **One big egg stays closed.** Mother sits longer, alone.

**Vocab**: nest / egg / mother / wait / open

### Beat 2 · Discovery — The Big Egg Cracks
- At last the great egg splits.
- Out comes a duckling — but he is **large and grey**, not yellow.
- The other ducklings stop playing. They stare.

**Vocab**: grey / different / look / stare / strange

### Beat 3 · Rejection — Farmyard Bullying
- The family visits the farmyard.
- Hens peck at him. Ducks chase him.
- His mother defends him at first, but then **turns her head away**.

**Vocab**: peck / chase / hide / cry / alone

### Beat 4 · Escape — Wild Ducks and the Gunshot
- He runs away across the fields.
- Wild ducks let him rest. A kind friend joins him.
- Suddenly, **gunshots crack** through the air. The friend falls. He runs.

**Vocab**: run / friend / shot / fall / hide

### Beat 5 · Cottage — Cat and Hen Bully
- He finds a cottage with an old woman, a cat, and a hen.
- They keep him — but mock him for not laying eggs or purring.
- "What good are you?" He leaves into the cold.

**Vocab**: cottage / hen / cat / mock / leave

### Beat 6 · Winter — Frozen and Rescued
- He sees beautiful white birds flying south — he longs for them.
- Winter comes. He is **frozen in the ice**.
- A farmer rescues him; children frighten him; he flees again.

**Vocab**: winter / ice / frozen / farmer / fly

### Beat 7 · Reveal — He Is a Swan
- Spring comes. He sees the white birds again.
- He bends to drown himself in shame — but the water shows his reflection.
- **He is a swan.** Children call him the most beautiful of all.

**Vocab**: spring / swan / reflection / beautiful / new

## A2 Authoring Constraints (per pickup-item-writer skill)

- Stem ≤ 11 words
- listen-tf must use inference (atmosphere / action / time / contrast)
- correct option paraphrases passage (X3 anti-verbatim)
- B1+ vocab banned (no "majestic" / "gleaming" / "rejected" / "yearned")
- A2 replacements: majestic → great / yearned → wanted / rejected → pushed away

## Story Voice

- 3rd-person narrator (奶奶's voice)
- Past tense (was / went / saw / cried)
- Mochi + Hana 偶爾 react (e.g. Hana whimpers when duckling cries)

## Theme

- 不是「外貌歧視」是「不屬於這個群體 → 找到真正的家」
- 對 user 的 hook: 「沒有歸屬感」 / 「下班後再試一次」共鳴
