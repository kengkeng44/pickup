# Canon — Anansi the Spider (Anansi 蜘蛛 — 怎麼帶回所有故事)

> Source: West African oral tradition (Akan / Ashanti people of Ghana). Public domain — folk/oral. Episode chosen: **'Anansi gets all the stories from Sky God Nyame'** (蜘蛛從天神拿回所有故事的起源傳說).
> **NOT used**: Gerald McDermott 1972 picture book / Disney / any modern post-1929 retelling. No specific named retelling's added phrases retained.
> CEFR-A2 retelling for Pickup Ch21. Plot beats from oral tradition only; A2 self-paraphrased sentences (no specific translation copied).

## 7-Beat Arc (with key vocab)

### Beat 1 · Setup — A World With No Stories
- Long ago, there were no stories in the world.
- People sat together at night, and no one had a tale to tell.
- All the stories belonged to Nyame, the Sky God.
- A small spider named Anansi looked up at the sky.

**Vocab**: story / world / night / sky / spider

### Beat 2 · The Price — Three Animals
- Anansi went up a long thin thread to the sky.
- "I want to buy all your stories," he said to Nyame.
- "The price is high," Nyame said. "Bring me three animals."
- Anansi looked at the list. "I can do this," he said.

**Vocab**: thread / buy / price / high / list

### Beat 3 · The Hornets in the Pot
- The first animal was a swarm of hornets.
- Anansi cut a big leaf and held it over his head like a roof.
- He filled a pot with water and poured some on the hornet tree.
- "The rain is coming! Quick, get in my pot!" the hornets flew inside.

**Vocab**: hornet / leaf / roof / pour / pot

### Beat 4 · The Python and the Stick
- The second animal was a long python.
- Anansi found a strong stick and walked to the python.
- "My friend says you are not really that long," Anansi said.
- The python lay down by the stick to show his length, and Anansi tied him fast.

**Vocab**: python / stick / friend / long / tie

### Beat 5 · The Leopard in the Hole
- The third animal was a leopard with sharp eyes.
- Anansi dug a deep hole on the path the leopard liked.
- That night the leopard fell into the hole and could not climb out.
- "I will help you up," Anansi said, and pulled him out with a long rope.

**Vocab**: leopard / dig / hole / fall / rope

### Beat 6 · The Sky God Pays Up
- Anansi brought the three animals to Nyame in the sky.
- The Sky God looked at the hornets, the python, and the leopard.
- "You are small, but your head is big," Nyame said with a smile.
- He gave Anansi a wooden box full of all the stories of the world.

**Vocab**: bring / look / head / box / world

### Beat 7 · Stories Fly Out
- Anansi carried the heavy box back down to the ground.
- He opened the lid in the middle of the village.
- The stories flew out like birds and went to every corner of the earth.
- And that is why, even today, there is a story in every home.

**Vocab**: carry / open / lid / fly / home

## A2 Authoring Constraints

- Stem ≤ 11 words
- X3 anti-verbatim: correct option paraphrases the passage
- listen-tf must use inference (atmosphere / action / contrast)
- A2 child-friendly replacements:
  - ❌ `killed` / `stung to death` → ✅ `gave up and went home` / `flew away`
  - ❌ `swarm of angry hornets` → ✅ `a group of hornets`
  - ❌ `strangled` → ✅ `tied him up`
  - ❌ `trapped` (verb) → ✅ `caught` / `held safely`
  - ❌ `cunning` → ✅ `smart` / `clever`
  - ❌ `Nyame the Sky God` allowed (proper noun, kept once for cultural anchor; otherwise 'the Sky God')
  - ❌ `python` / `leopard` / `hornet` allowed at A2 with picture context (per task brief)

## Story Voice

- 3rd-person past tense storytelling (grandma reading aloud to Mochi + Hana)
- Plain A2 English. No archaic vocab.
- No blood, no horror. The hornets / python / leopard all just **'gave up and went home'** or **'were caught safely'** — no animal is killed.
- Theme is **智取** (outwit by brains, not strength) — pairs with Ch19 Sang Kancil.
- No Chinese (Hanzi) inside sentences / options / question prompts pre-reveal.

## Theme

- 「世界上的故事是誰的?為什麼每個家都聽得到?」
- 小蜘蛛用聰明,不用力量,贏過大動物 — Anansi 的 trickster archetype.
- 智取 + 想像力 + 起源神話 (etiological tale) — A2-friendly 對 8-12 兒童 + 親子家庭客群高度切題。
- 0 風險: 沒有血腥, 沒有死亡, 三個動物都是 'gave up and went home',結局是世界從此有故事。
- 文化分佈 bear band 'Other (Africa)' coverage (per pickup-story-standards.md §4).
