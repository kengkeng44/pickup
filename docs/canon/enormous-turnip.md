# Canon — The Enormous Turnip (蘿蔔大冒險)

> Source: Russian folktale, oral tradition (Repka / Репка). Recorded by
> Afanasyev 1863. Public domain worldwide. Cumulative folktale structure
> — each character adds, repeats expand.
> CEFR-A2 retelling for Pickup Ch20.

## 7-Beat Arc (cumulative structure — one new character per beat)

### Beat 1 · Setup — Grandpa Plants a Turnip
- Grandpa plants a small turnip seed in the garden.
- He gives it water every day. He sings to it.
- The turnip grows. It grows bigger and bigger.
- One day, it is the biggest turnip in the village.

**Vocab**: grandpa / seed / water / grow / big

### Beat 2 · The First Pull — Grandpa Alone
- Grandpa comes to pull the turnip out.
- He holds the green leaves with both hands.
- He pulls and pulls. The turnip will not move.
- Grandpa is one. The turnip is too big for one.

**Vocab**: pull / leaves / hands / move / one

### Beat 3 · Calling Grandma — Two Pulling
- Grandpa calls, "Grandma, come help me!"
- Grandma comes. She holds Grandpa's coat.
- Grandpa pulls the turnip. Grandma pulls Grandpa.
- They both pull. The turnip will not move.

**Vocab**: grandma / call / help / coat / both

### Beat 4 · Calling Granddaughter — Three Pulling
- Grandma calls, "Granddaughter, come help us!"
- The little girl runs out. She holds Grandma's apron.
- Grandpa pulls. Grandma pulls. Granddaughter pulls.
- Three together. The turnip will not move.

**Vocab**: granddaughter / run / apron / three / together

### Beat 5 · Calling the Dog — Still Not Enough
- Granddaughter calls, "Little dog, come help us!"
- The dog runs over. He holds her dress with his teeth.
- Four pulling now: Grandpa, Grandma, Granddaughter, Dog.
- They pull and pull. The turnip will not move.

**Vocab**: dog / over / teeth / dress / four

### Beat 6 · Calling the Cat — Almost There
- The dog calls, "Cat, come help us!"
- The cat walks over. She holds the dog's tail.
- Five pulling now. Everyone pulls hard.
- The turnip moves a little. But not enough.

**Vocab**: cat / walk / tail / five / enough

### Beat 7 · The Little Mouse — Out It Comes!
- The cat calls, "Little mouse, come help us!"
- The mouse runs out. She holds the cat's tail.
- Six together. They pull one more time.
- Out comes the turnip! The whole family shares a big dinner.

**Vocab**: mouse / six / together / out / share

## A2 Authoring Constraints

- Stem ≤ 11 words
- X3 anti-verbatim: correct option paraphrases the passage
- listen-tf must use inference (size of turnip / why one is not enough /
  why even small mouse matters)
- B1+ vocab banned: yanked / heaved / strained / enormous (→ 'very big' /
  'bigger than the house') / persistent / determined
- A2 replacements: enormous → very big / yanked → pulled hard /
  pulled with all his might → pulled and pulled
- 0 violence / 0 death / 0 conflict — only family teamwork

## Story Voice

- Cumulative rhythm — every new pull lists everyone in order
- Repeating refrain: "They pulled and pulled. The turnip would not move."
  (until the very last pull, when it DOES move)
- A2 child-friendly: short sentences, simple verbs, present tense
- Repetition is the teaching tool — A2 children memorize through pattern

## Theme

- 一個人不夠就找全家 — strength in togetherness
- 連最小的也有用 — even the smallest mouse tips the balance
- 100% 溫馨家庭場景, 0 conflict / 0 violence
- 對 user 的 hook: 「再小的幫忙都重要」共鳴
