## A. Angle research (5 bullet)

- **"Try before told" pedagogy** — Brilliant presents an interactive problem BEFORE any explanation. The learner interacts first, forms a prediction, then sees the concept revealed. Discovery learning = 2× retention vs passive read (Kapur 2016 productive failure).
- **Micro-step scaffolding** — every complex task is broken into the smallest possible unit with feedback at each micro-step, not just the final answer. Drag a slider → see live result. No "submit all at once."
- **User-paced advance** — Brilliant NEVER auto-advances. The learner controls when to move on. No countdown timers, no forced dwell. Respects variation in reading speed and cognition load.
- **Concept mastery map** — completion screen names the specific concepts mastered (e.g. "You've mastered: Geometric Sequences, Ratios"). Not just XP / accuracy / time — named knowledge.
- **Visual interaction affordances** — tap areas are clearly labeled with what will happen ("Tap to reveal", "Drag to explore"). No ambiguity about what a tap will do before you tap it.

## B. Pickup 現狀 (對齊這 angle)

- **NarrationRenderer auto-advances** — `onEnd` callback fires after TTS, then waits 2s and calls `onAdvance`. User has zero control. If audio fails (iOS first-tap, low bandwidth), they are stuck waiting for a 7–14s fallback timer. Brilliant angle: no user-driven continue affordance.
- **TapTilesRenderer zero scaffolding** — user is shown shuffled word tiles with no hint of expected output length or structure. The "sentence slot" area shows "選字排這裡…" with no word count. A12 learner has no mental model of the sentence length before starting. Brilliant angle: missing micro-step guidance.
- **TapPairsRenderer matched items dim to opacity:0.5** — successfully matched pairs go visually muted. Looks like an error state or "used up" rather than a success. Brilliant angle: celebration of each micro-match should feel positive, not suppressive.
- **CompletePanel stat triptych = XP / Accuracy / Time** — no named vocabulary list or concept map. Learner can't see WHAT they learned, only how they performed. Brilliant angle: mastery summary is opaque.
- **ListenTfRenderer blanks display = all `____` tokens** — `blanks(en)` replaces every word with `____`. A sentence like "The rain falls hard tonight" becomes `____ ____ ____ ____ ____`. Zero structural info. Brilliant angle: users need at least word-count or length hints to scaffold listening attempts.
