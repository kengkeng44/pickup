## A. Angle research (Duolingo streak/league patterns)

- **Streak Freeze** (shield): one free freeze/week, extra purchasable with gems. Removes "start over at 1" cliff. Duolingo internal data: churn -21% cohort who use freeze vs those who don't. Pickup master matrix marks this highest-ROI post-narration feature.
- **Streak Repair**: if yesterday was missed (gap=1 full day), user sees "Repair your streak? Use 10 gems" prompt on next open. 24-hour window. Prevents permanent loss from a single forgotten day.
- **Daily XP goal**: user sets 5/10/20/30 XP target. HUD shows daily progress toward goal. Triggers streak if goal hit. Removes ambiguity ("did I do enough today?").
- **Leagues**: 7-day weekly leaderboard, 30 users per tier (Bronze→Diamond). XP during week feeds rank. Top 10 promote, bottom 5 demote. Creates social FOMO + weekly rhythm.
- **Streak milestone celebrations**: Duolingo fires full-screen confetti + animated mascot + share card at 7/14/30/100-day milestones. "Weekly Resilience" badge equivalent.
