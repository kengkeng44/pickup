## A. Angle research (5 bullet)

- **Thumb zone law**: On 6"+ phones (iPhone 15 = 6.12"), the comfortable one-handed reach zone is the lower ~40% of the screen. Instagram pins ALL primary actions (Like / Comment / Share / Mute / Follow) to the lower-right quadrant — users never need to stretch to the top.
- **Minimum tap target**: Apple HIG = 44×44pt; Google Material = 48×48dp. Instagram's tap targets average 52×52dp — deliberately larger than spec to reduce misfire on fast casual scrolling.
- **Dismiss-from-bottom pattern**: Instagram Reels close button is swipe-down, not a ✕ at top-left. Top-left requires re-grip on large phones. Industry trend: primary dismiss = bottom gesture or lower-right corner.
- **No hidden-behind-nav content**: Instagram's scroll container always has `padding-bottom` equal to the tab bar height + safe-area, so the last content item is never occluded by the fixed nav.
- **Word tile games (Stories "Polls")**: Tap targets on word-chips are inflated to 44pt min — never let a chip be narrower than 44px even if the text is 2 characters. Misfire on tiny tiles breaks flow and kills engagement.

## B. Pickup 現狀 (對齊 thumb-reach angle)

- **SpeakerBtn default size=22px** (renderers.tsx:84): Default `size=22` means the hit area is 22×22px — 50% below HIG minimum. Callsites at lines 176 (narration) and 242 (cloze sentence) use this too-small default. Only listen-mc (size=48), type-what-you-hear (size=48), and tap-tiles (size=44) pass correctly.
- **Tap-tile word chips are too narrow**: TapTilesRenderer chips use `padding: '8px 14px'` with no `minWidth` or `minHeight`. Short words ("I", "a", "to", "in") render ~30px tall × ~34px wide — well below 44×44px. Misfire risk on mobile high.
- **Fixed BottomNav ≈ 65px tall, but page padding-bottom = only 24px**: TasksPage, ProfilePage, AlertsPage all use `padding: '16px 14px 24px'`. The fixed nav bar = 8+8+28+10+env(safe-area) ≈ 65-99px. Bottom content is occluded behind the nav on iPhone (especially with home bar).
- **Lesson close ✕ is top-left**: On iPhone 15 Pro Max (6.7"), top-left requires either re-grip or two-handed use. Instagram / Duolingo both use swipe-down or lower-positioned dismiss. The ✕ at top-left is the single most unreachable tap target in the lesson flow.
- **Key-sentences trigger button (MapPage) is 38×38px**: The paw icon button in the chapter header card is `width:38, height:38` — 6px short of HIG 44px minimum.
