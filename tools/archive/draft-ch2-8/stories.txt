CH1 - 「我跟你的第一天」 完整 8 題版
================================================================

故事流程:
  Q1-Q3  Prologue(3 題)   建立世界:糰糰 / 花花 / 奶奶說故事
  Q4-Q6  奶奶的故事(3 題)  雨夜遇貓 / 撐傘 / 帶回家
  Q7     Closing(1 題)    Goodnight
  Q8     Review(1 題)     4 個關鍵字配對

每題類型分散:listen-mc x2 / listen-emoji x1 / type x2 / listen-comprehension x1 / tap-tiles x1 / tap-pairs x1


--------------------------------------------------------------
Q1 [listen-mc]
--------------------------------------------------------------
storyBeat: 我是糰糰,跟奶奶住
sentence: "I am 糰糰. I am a small cat."
question: "Which word did you hear?"
options: ["cat", "cap", "cut", "cot"]
correctIndex: 0
explanationZh:
  cat = 貓。cap(帽子)、cut(剪)、cot(嬰兒床)母音相近,
  要靠尾音跟前面的母音長度分辨。糰糰是一隻三花貓。


--------------------------------------------------------------
Q2 [listen-emoji]
--------------------------------------------------------------
storyBeat: 花花是柴犬,我的朋友
sentence: "花花 is my friend. He is a brown dog."
question: "Who is 花花?"
options: ["🐕 a dog", "🐈 a cat", "🦅 a bird", "🐇 a rabbit"]
correctIndex: 0
explanationZh:
  花花是 brown dog(棕色的狗)— 一隻柴犬。雖然花花聽起來像
  花的名字,但他是奶奶後來撿到的小狗,現在是糰糰最好的朋友。


--------------------------------------------------------------
Q3 [type-what-you-hear]
--------------------------------------------------------------
storyBeat: 每晚奶奶都說一個故事
sentence: "Tonight Grandma tells a story."
question: "Type the missing word"
correct answer: story
explanationZh:
  story = 故事。奶奶的女兒跟孫子住得遠不常來,所以每晚奶奶
  都對糰糰跟花花說一個 story。今天說的是糰糰自己的故事。


--------------------------------------------------------------
Q4 [listen-comprehension] ← 奶奶開始說故事
--------------------------------------------------------------
storyBeat: 從前一個雨夜,奶奶看見一隻小貓
sentence: "Long ago, on a rainy night, I saw a small cat."
question: "What did Grandma see?"
options:
  - "A small cat"      ✓
  - "A big dog"
  - "A red bird"
  - "An old tree"
explanationZh:
  long ago = 很久以前。奶奶說她在 a rainy night(雨夜)看見了
  a small cat 在路上 — 那就是糰糰跟奶奶相遇的起點。


--------------------------------------------------------------
Q5 [type-what-you-hear]
--------------------------------------------------------------
storyBeat: 奶奶撐開傘給濕透的小貓
sentence: "I opened my umbrella for her."
question: "Type the missing word"
correct answer: umbrella
explanationZh:
  umbrella = 傘。長一點的字,3 個音節 um-brel-la。奶奶為了
  濕透的小貓撐開傘 — 這是糰糰跟奶奶第一次靠近。


--------------------------------------------------------------
Q6 [tap-tiles]
--------------------------------------------------------------
storyBeat: 奶奶把小貓帶回家
sentence: "I took her home."
question: "Tap what you hear"
tiles: ["I", "took", "her", "home", "saw", "house"]
correct order: I → took → her → home
explanationZh:
  依序點:I → took → her → home。took 是 take 的過去式
  (帶過去了)。saw 跟 house 是干擾。house 跟 home 意思相近但
  語感不同 — home 是「家」,有家人感情。


--------------------------------------------------------------
Q7 [listen-mc] ← 糰糰結尾
--------------------------------------------------------------
storyBeat: 故事說完了,糰糰跟奶奶說晚安
sentence: "Goodnight, Grandma."
question: "Which word did you hear?"
options: ["Goodnight", "Good morning", "Good day", "Goodbye"]
correctIndex: 0
explanationZh:
  Goodnight = 晚安(睡覺前說的)。Goodbye 是再見、Good morning
  是早安、Good day 是日安。糰糰聽完故事舔舔腳,跟奶奶說 Goodnight。


--------------------------------------------------------------
Q8 [tap-pairs] ← Ch1 章節 review
--------------------------------------------------------------
storyBeat: 今晚的四個關鍵字
sentence: "These are tonight's four words."
pairs:
  cat       貓
  umbrella  傘
  rainy     下雨的
  home      家
explanationZh:
  糰糰的故事四個關鍵字:cat 貓、umbrella 傘、rainy 下雨的、
  home 家。它們組成了奶奶遇到糰糰的那個雨夜。

CH1 narrative 文字版(讀起來像繪本一篇)
================================================================

I am 糰糰. I am a small cat.
我是糰糰,一隻小貓。

花花 is my friend. He is a brown dog.
花花是我的朋友,一隻棕色的狗。

Tonight Grandma tells a story.
今晚奶奶要說故事。

"Long ago, on a rainy night,
 I saw a small cat.
 從前一個雨夜,
 我看見一隻小貓。

 I opened my umbrella for her.
 我為她撐開傘。

 I took her home."
 我把她帶回家。

Goodnight, Grandma.
晚安,奶奶。

(全文 8 句英文 / 8 句中文。長度剛好讀完一頁繪本。)
